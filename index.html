Hi! I'm a front-end developer from St.Petersburg, Russia. I have strong knowledge of HTML5/CSS3, JavaScript and jQuery. I have experience in developing and testing cross-browser layouts and in creating responsive web pages using mobile-first approach. I make websites as close to the templates as possible and always care about layout flexibility and the convenience of its further use and development. My favorite tools are Jade and LESS together with Grunt. I'm familiar with different front-end naming methodologies (personally I prefer BEM with adjustments by Nicolas Gallagher). I have also basic knowledge of SEO and of user interface design (UX/UI).
